⚙️ How Puzzle Extraction Works
1

Engine receives position

Every position in a finished engine game is fed to Stockfish via UCI protocol. The position is set with position fen ….

2

Deep search — depth 50

Stockfish searches the game tree to depth 50, spending up to 30s or 25M nodes per position — matching Lichess's own quality threshold.

go depth 50 movetime 30000 nodes 25000000
3

Win-chance swing detected

If the position's win% drops by ≥ 30 percentage points vs the previous position, it's flagged as a blunder — a potential puzzle moment.

Δwin ≥ 0.30 & win% ≥ 0.50
4

Uniqueness check

MultiPV=3 is used. The puzzle is rejected if the second-best move is within 50cp of the best — only positions with a single clear best response qualify.

best₁ − best₂ > 50cp
5

Theme tagging (70 themes)

cook.js replays the solution and detects tactical motifs: fork, pin, skewer, mate patterns, endgame types, zugzwang, promotion, and 60+ more.

6

Saved to MongoDB

The puzzle document gets a cg_ prefixed ID, status pending, and enters the review queue. Approved puzzles enter training rotation.
